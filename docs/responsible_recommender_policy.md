# Responsible recommender policy

This repository is designed for research and system review.

## Do not use this lab to

- automatically suppress or boost real creators
- rank real users or infer sensitive user traits
- make high-stakes personalization decisions
- replace legal, privacy, platform-policy, or stakeholder review
- deploy recommender outputs without monitoring and appeal mechanisms

## Required real-world controls

Any institutional or production use should include:

- privacy and data-minimization review
- user controls and transparency notices
- provider/creator appeal channels
- fairness monitoring over time
- content safety review where relevant
- human governance for metric tradeoffs
- documentation of ranking objectives and known limitations

## Interpretation

Fairness and trust metrics in this lab are descriptive indicators. They should guide investigation, not automate final decisions.
